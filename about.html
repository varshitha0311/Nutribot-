About FoodAI

A deep-learning powered food recognition system built to help you make healthier choices — especially for people managing diabetes.

Our Mission

FoodAI combines state-of-the-art CNNs (Custom CNN, SqueezeNet, and VGG16) to instantly classify 12 popular food items from a single photo. We then provide detailed nutritional information and diabetic-friendly advice so you can enjoy food guilt-free.

Our goal is to empower individuals with diabetes to make informed dietary choices through the power of AI, reducing the guesswork and promoting a balanced lifestyle.

How It Works

  • 01 Upload any food image
  • 02 VGG16 model (84.67% accuracy) detects the dish
  • 03 Get calories, macros & smart tips instantly

Our Technology

PyTorch

Deep learning framework for model training & inference.

VGG16 / SqueezeNet

Pretrained CNNs fine-tuned on our food dataset.

Food-101 Subset

12 classes, 10,000+ images, carefully curated.

Behind the Scenes

Training Data

  • 12 food categories, 800–1000 images each
  • Split: 80% train, 10% validation, 10% test
  • Data augmentation: rotation, flip, zoom

Model Training

  • Transfer learning with ImageNet weights
  • Optimizer: Adam, LR=0.0001, batch size=32
  • Early stopping, 50 epochs

Why Accurate Food Recognition Matters

For people with diabetes, knowing carbohydrate content is crucial. Our AI provides instant estimates, helping users manage blood sugar levels and make better dietary choices. With 84.67% accuracy, we're one step closer to reliable automated food logging.
