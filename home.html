Why FoodAI for Diabetes?

Over 422 million people worldwide have diabetes. Managing blood sugar starts with knowing what's on your plate. FoodAI gives you:

  • Instant carbohydrate estimates
  • Diabetic-friendly alternatives
  • Portion guidance based on AI
  • Education on glycemic impact

Did You Know?

🥧 A slice of apple pie has ~37g carbs – equivalent to 2.5 slices of bread.

🍟 French fries have a high glycemic index; pairing with protein helps.

🧀 Cheese is low-carb but high in saturated fat – moderation is key.

🍦 Ice cream can be enjoyed if you account for carbs and choose sugar-free options.
