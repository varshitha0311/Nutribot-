Training & Validation Accuracy

VGG16 converged fastest and achieved highest validation accuracy with minimal overfitting.

Custom CNN

5 convolutional layers with batch norm and max pooling, followed by 2 fully connected layers. Trained from scratch on our dataset.

SqueezeNet

Uses fire modules to reduce parameters while maintaining accuracy. Pretrained on ImageNet, fine-tuned on our 12 classes.

VGG16

16-layer deep network with small 3x3 filters. Pretrained on ImageNet, we replaced the classifier and fine-tuned all layers.
